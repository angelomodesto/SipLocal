# Wireframe Descriptions

This document describes the visual layout and structure of each page in the SipLocal application, based on the Yelp-inspired design.

## Design Principles

- **Clean and Modern:** Minimal clutter, easy to scan
- **Card-Based Layout:** Information organized in cards
- **Yelp-Inspired:** Similar visual style but unique branding
- **Responsive:** Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop
- **Interactive:** Clear buttons, hover states, smooth transitions

## Color Scheme and Styling

### Colors (Suggested):
- **Primary:** Coffee brown (#8B4513) or warm beige (#F5E6D3)
- **Accent:** Coffee cream (#FFF8DC) or warm orange (#FF8C00)
- **Text:** Dark gray (#333333) for body, black for headings
- **Background:** White (#FFFFFF) for cards, light gray (#F5F5F5) for page background
- **Success:** Green for positive ratings
- **Warning:** Yellow/Orange for medium ratings

### Typography:
- **Headings:** Bold, larger font (24-32px)
- **Body:** Regular weight, readable size (16px)
- **Labels:** Medium weight, smaller (14px)
- **Font Family:** Sans-serif, modern (system fonts or Google Fonts)

### Spacing:
- **Card Padding:** 16-24px
- **Card Gap:** 16-24px between cards
- **Section Padding:** 32-48px between major sections
- **Button Padding:** 12-16px vertical, 24px horizontal

### Interactive Elements:
- **Hover States:** Subtle shadow or color change
- **Click States:** Slight scale or color change
- **Loading States:** Skeleton screens or spinners
- **Transitions:** Smooth 0.2-0.3s transitions
